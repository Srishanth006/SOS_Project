About the Prototype

The core of our device is a custom-designed circuit built for reliability and low power consumption.

View Circuit Diagram (Drive Link)
  • ESP32 Microcontroller
  • GPS Module (NEO-6M)
  • Push Button (Tactile Switch)
  • LiPo Battery & Charger Module
  • 3D Printed/Custom Jhumka Casing
  • Connecting Wires

Our prototype was developed with affordability in mind to ensure accessibility.

Total Estimated Cost: Approximately ₹1500 - ₹2000 per unit.
